ADAPTIVE BDD TRAINING MODEL (ABTM) RESEARCH STUDY
Post-Training Semi-Structured Interview Protocol
=================================================================

Interviewer: _______________
Participant ID: _______________
Date: _______________
Interview Duration: ___________ minutes

INTERVIEW GUIDELINES:
- Duration: 30-45 minutes
- Location: Private, quiet space (or video call)
- Recording: Audio recorded with participant consent
- Approach: Semi-structured (follow-up questions encouraged)
- Tone: Conversational and non-judgmental

OPENING SCRIPT:
"Thank you for agreeing to participate in this interview. The purpose 
is to understand your experience with the Adaptive BDD Training Model. 
There are no right or wrong answers - I'm interested in your honest 
opinions and experiences. With your permission, I'd like to audio 
record this conversation for transcription purposes. The recording will 
be kept confidential and used only for research purposes. Do I have 
your permission to record?"

☐ Permission granted  ☐ Permission denied

-----------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION A: BACKGROUND AND PRIOR EXPERIENCE (5 minutes)
-----------------------------------------------------------------

1. Can you briefly describe your role and experience with software 
   development before this training?

   Probe: Years of experience? Team size? Agile experience?

   _________________________________________________________________

2. What was your prior knowledge of BDD before starting this training?

   Probe: Had you heard of it? Used it? What did you know about it?

   _________________________________________________________________

3. What motivated you to participate in this BDD training?

   Probe: Personal interest? Team requirement? Career goals?

   _________________________________________________________________

-----------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION B: LEARNING EXPERIENCE (10 minutes)
-----------------------------------------------------------------

4. How would you describe your overall experience with the training?

   Probe: Positive? Challenging? Engaging?

   _________________________________________________________________

5. What aspects of the training did you find most helpful?

   Probe: Specific modules? Hands-on practice? Examples? Feedback?

   _________________________________________________________________

6. What aspects of the training were most challenging?

   Probe: Concepts? Gherkin syntax? Practical application?

   _________________________________________________________________

7. The training used an adaptive approach that adjusted based on 
   your performance. How did you experience this adaptability?

   Probe: Did you notice adjustments? Helpful? Frustrating? 
         Examples of adaptations?

   _________________________________________________________________

8. How effective was the personalized feedback you received on your 
   scenario submissions?

   Probe: Clarity of feedback? Timeliness? Helpfulness? Specificity?

   _________________________________________________________________

9. Were there any topics or areas where you felt you needed more 
   support or practice?

   Probe: What additional resources would have helped?

   _________________________________________________________________

-----------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION C: SKILL DEVELOPMENT (10 minutes)
-----------------------------------------------------------------

10. How confident do you feel now in writing BDD scenarios compared 
    to before the training?

    Probe: Rate on 1-10 scale? Specific improvements?

    _________________________________________________________________

11. Can you give me an example of something you learned that changed 
    how you think about requirements or testing?

    Probe: Specific "aha" moments? New perspectives?

    _________________________________________________________________

12. How has the training affected your understanding of Gherkin 
    syntax and best practices?

    Probe: Common mistakes learned to avoid? Improved quality?

    _________________________________________________________________

13. Do you feel the training prepared you to use BDD in real projects?

    Probe: What makes you feel prepared/unprepared?

    _________________________________________________________________

-----------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION D: COLLABORATION AND COMMUNICATION (8 minutes)
-----------------------------------------------------------------

14. [If applicable] How has the training affected how you communicate 
    with other team members (developers, testers, product owners)?

    Probe: Shared language? Clearer communication?

    _________________________________________________________________

15. Do you think BDD scenarios could improve collaboration on your 
    team? Why or why not?

    Probe: How specifically? Challenges to adoption?

    _________________________________________________________________

16. How might BDD help bridge communication gaps between technical 
    and non-technical stakeholders?

    Probe: Examples? Potential benefits?

    _________________________________________________________________

-----------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION E: PRACTICAL APPLICATION (7 minutes)
-----------------------------------------------------------------

17. Do you plan to use BDD in your work after this training?

    Probe: Why or why not? What would enable/prevent you?

    _________________________________________________________________

18. What do you see as the main benefits of using BDD?

    Probe: Quality? Clarity? Automation? Communication?

    _________________________________________________________________

19. What do you see as the main challenges in adopting BDD?

    Probe: Learning curve? Team buy-in? Time? Tools?

    _________________________________________________________________

20. What support or resources would you need to successfully 
    implement BDD on your team?

    Probe: Tools? Training for others? Management support?

    _________________________________________________________________

-----------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION F: TRAINING MODEL EVALUATION (5 minutes)
-----------------------------------------------------------------

21. How did the adaptive/personalized nature of the training compare 
    to other technical training you've experienced?

    Probe: Better? Worse? Different? Why?

    _________________________________________________________________

22. What specific features of the training model did you find most 
    valuable?

    Probe: Role-specific content? Adaptive difficulty? Feedback?

    _________________________________________________________________

23. If you could change one thing about the training, what would it be?

    Probe: Content? Delivery? Pace? Practice opportunities?

    _________________________________________________________________

24. Would you recommend this training to others? Why or why not?

    Probe: Who would benefit most?

    _________________________________________________________________

-----------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION G: CLOSING (2-3 minutes)
-----------------------------------------------------------------

25. Is there anything else about your training experience that you'd 
    like to share?

    _________________________________________________________________

26. Do you have any questions for me about the research or the 
    training?

    _________________________________________________________________

CLOSING SCRIPT:
"Thank you so much for your time and for sharing your experiences. 
Your insights are very valuable for this research. If you think of 
anything else you'd like to add, please feel free to contact me. 
Have a great day!"
